Strava Friend Breaker

Friend Breaker (SFB) takes the competitiveness of Strava to a whole new level. SFB leverages the Strava API to analyze every segment you've ridden and looks for the segments where one of your friends is currently ranked ahead of you. Knowing how you rank among your friends allows you to target your efforts and identifies which segments you need to attack!

Because we all want to be better than our friends...
